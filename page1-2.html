CO-QUIT

"Co-" + "Quit"

Co-Quit is a name made by a combination of Co- means together and QUIT.
Through Co-Quit, users can help their loved ones to quit smoking or practice quitting smoking.

COLOR TONE

Co-Quit uses a different color tone depending on the user's role.
Non-smokers used pure and clean colors, while non-smokers used high colors to alert non-smokers.

INTERFACE

With Co-Quit, smokers can be directly managed by non-smokers.
As a helper, non-smokers encourage smokers to stop smoking by sending messages of support or sometimes by nagging them.
