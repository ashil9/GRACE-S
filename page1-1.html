MYHO

"MAKE YOUR HOBBY"

MYHO is short for MAKE YOUR HOBBY, which means that people can create their own hobbies or find new hobbies.
Myho's icon is shaped like a sprout made of ribbon. Ribbon means hand-made handicraft, sprout means learning.

COLOR TONE

Myho's design is composed entirely of dark navy, purple and white tones.
These colors gices a calm, reliable professional feel while delivering a mysterious image.

INTERFACE

Myho's title logo used a lively font and valued readability.
The UI was designed around usability so that even first-time users do not have difficulties.
